The practice is open **Monday to Friday 8am–7pm** and **Saturday 8am–12pm**.

<p class="text-small">Sunday: Closed. We are closed on all bank holidays and public holidays in England and Wales.</p>

## How to Book

To book an appointment, please call us on **<a href="tel:01525237444">01525 237444</a>** or email <a href="mailto:reception@heathandreachvets.co.uk">reception@heathandreachvets.co.uk</a>.

You can also book appointments online using our booking system:

<p><a href="/online/" class="btn btn-amber">Book Online</a></p>

Our phone lines have an automatic queuing system — your call will be answered in turn, so please stay on the line.
