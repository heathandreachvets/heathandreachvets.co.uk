We offer a comprehensive range of veterinary services for dogs, cats and small mammals, all delivered with personal care and attention.

<div class="service-list">

### Consultations

Routine and urgent appointments with our experienced veterinary team.

### Vaccinations

Primary courses and annual boosters for dogs and cats.

### Neutering

Routine spaying and castration for dogs, cats and rabbits.

### Surgery

Soft tissue and orthopaedic procedures in our fully equipped operating theatre.

### Dental Care

Dental examinations, scaling, polishing and extractions under general anaesthetic.

### Diagnostic Imaging

Digital radiography (X-ray) and ultrasound for accurate diagnosis.

### Laboratory Testing

In-house blood testing for rapid results, plus external laboratory services.

### ECG

Electrocardiography for heart assessment.

### Microchipping

Permanent identification for your pet, a legal requirement for dogs in the UK.

### Nurse Clinics

Weight management, post-operative checks, nail clipping and general health advice.

### Repeat Prescriptions

Convenient repeat prescription service for ongoing medications. <a href="/prescriptions/">Request a repeat prescription</a>.
